# Using the guided filing wizard

The guided filing wizard helps you prepare your annual return step by step. Here's how to use it:

## Starting the wizard

1. Go to Filing Wizard
2. Select the tax year
3. Click "Start Wizard"
4. Follow the steps

## Wizard steps

### Step 1: Confirm business details

- Review your business information
- Update if needed
- Confirm and proceed

### Step 2: Income

- Review income transactions
- Add any missing income
- Verify totals
- Proceed to expenses

### Step 3: Expenses

- Review expense transactions
- Add any missing expenses
- Attach receipts where needed
- Verify totals

### Step 4: Evidence

- Review attached documents
- Add missing receipts
- Ensure all business expenses have evidence

### Step 5: Review

- Review all information
- Check calculations
- Make final adjustments
- Complete the wizard

## Tips

- **Take your time**: Don't rush through the wizard. Review each step carefully.
- **Have documents ready**: Gather receipts and documents before starting.
- **Save progress**: You can save and return to the wizard later.
- **Get help**: Use the help icons on each step for guidance.

## After completing

- **Generate filing pack**: Use the wizard data to generate your filing pack
- **Review**: Review the generated pack
- **Submit**: Submit to FIRS (or share with your accountant)

## Common questions

**Q: Can I skip steps?**
A: No, but you can save and return later. Complete all steps for accurate filing.

**Q: What if I make a mistake?**
A: You can go back to previous steps and make changes.

**Q: Do I have to complete in one session?**
A: No, you can save progress and return later.

**Q: Is the wizard required?**
A: No, but it helps ensure accuracy. You can generate filing packs without it.
