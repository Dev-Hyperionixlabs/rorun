# How to complete onboarding

Getting started with Rorun takes just a few minutes. Here's how to complete onboarding:

## Step 1: Sign up

1. Go to the signup page
2. Enter your phone number
3. Verify with the OTP code sent to your phone
4. Enter your name and email (optional)

## Step 2: Create your business

1. Enter your business name
2. Select your legal form (e.g., Limited Liability Company, Sole Proprietorship)
3. Choose your sector
4. Enter your state
5. Add your CAC number (if you have one)
6. Add your TIN (if you have one)
7. Indicate if you're VAT registered
8. Estimate your annual turnover band

## Step 3: Review your tax status

After completing onboarding, Rorun will:
- Calculate your tax obligations (CIT, VAT, WHT)
- Show you your obligations dashboard
- Set up reminders for upcoming deadlines

## Tips

- **Be accurate**: The more accurate your information, the better Rorun can calculate your obligations.
- **You can update later**: Don't worry if you don't have all the information right away. You can update your business profile anytime.
- **Add transactions**: Start adding transactions to get the full benefit of Rorun's tracking and filing pack features.

That's it! You're ready to use Rorun.
